Chain of Thought vs Latent Thought — Key Points
July 19, 2026

Both reasoning paradigms reuse the same Transformer block in a loop — they differ only in what gets fed back each step. That one difference is why latent thought is good at parallel reasoning and CoT is good at approximate counting.
